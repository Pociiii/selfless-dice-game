How It Works

The Factory produces Sex Devices over time. Each Sex Device costs XGold to produce.

The Market checks for sales every few seconds. Each check has a chance to sell items based on Demand.

Speed → produces faster
Advertising → increases demand
Logistics → sells more often
Factory Tier → increases item cost, item value and item output.

Produce → Sell → Earn → Upgrade → Expand → Repeat.

Rebranding (Prestige) lets you reset your empire in exchange for Influence, a permanent bonus that speeds up future production. The more devices you sell in a run, the more Influence you gain.
