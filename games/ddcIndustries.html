How It Works

The Factory produces Sex Devices automatically over time. Each production cycle consumes XGold to manufacture Sex Devices.

The Market checks for sales at regular intervals. Each check attempts to sell devices based on Market Demand and your current Sales Capacity.

Use the + buttons next to stats to improve your operation:
Cost Reduction → lowers production cost
Production Time → produces devices faster
Market Demand → improves sale success and increases sales volume
Sale Check → processes sales more frequently

Rebranding (Prestige) allows you to reset your operation in exchange for Influence, representing your brand power and market reach. Influence improves sales efficiency by increasing sale success and boosting extra sales chances. The more devices you sell in a run, the more Influence you gain.
