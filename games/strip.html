Strip Game

Rules

  • Everyone start with 4 pieces of clothing, and can only have max 4 pieces
  • Stand in circle
  • /roll6 when it's your turn
  • Use the outcome table to determine your action
  • Once a player is naked, they dance in the middle of the circle

Outcome

Roll Action
1 Remove one item of clothing on yourself and show off
2 Remove clothing from player on your right, and tease them
3 Remove clothing from player on your left, and tease them
4 Swap 2 other player positions
5 Swap positions with someone else
6 Add clothing on yourself and flex
